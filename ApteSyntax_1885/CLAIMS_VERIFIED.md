## Methodology synthesis — are the author's presentation principles justified?

The seed's early reading confirms the programme's central finding from a new genre: Apte's factual accuracy is high, and where he differs from the other grammars the difference is CALIBRATION, not truth. The two cleanest contrasts are direct.
