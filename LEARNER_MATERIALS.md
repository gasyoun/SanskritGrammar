# Sanskrit learner materials in this repo — the learning ladder

_Created: 08-07-2026 · Last updated: 08-07-2026_

Which works in this repo serve **Sanskrit students**, at what stage, and in what language.
Companion to the archive listing in
[`README.md`](https://github.com/gasyoun/SanskritGrammar/blob/main/README.md) (which groups
by directory/format); this one orders them as the **pedagogical ladder MG uses** — Russian
at the base, German/English at the advanced end.

Every work below is fully digitized as `.mdx` and rendered on the
[Docusaurus site](https://gasyoun.github.io/SanskritGrammar/) (deploy target moving to
samskrtam.ru).

## Stage 1 — Start (complete beginner, from day one)

Used together from the very beginning:

| Work | Author · year | Lang | What it is | Read |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| ***Учебник санскрита*** | В. А. Кочергина · 1994 (repo: 6-е изд. 2017, ред. Н. П. Лихушина) | RU | The standard Russian university **textbook** — graded lessons + grammar + chrestomathy + glossary; built for taught **or self-study** use, startable without devanāgarī. THE beginner course. | [Kochergina](https://gasyoun.github.io/SanskritGrammar/grammars/KocherginaUchebnik_1998/Kochergina_unicode) |
| ***Конспект*** (грамматики санскрита) | А. А. Зализняк · 2004 | RU | A dense **conspectus / synopsis** used **alongside Kochergina from the start** — the compact grammar map beside the full course. | [Zaliznyak Конспект](https://gasyoun.github.io/SanskritGrammar/grammars/ZalizniakKonspekt_2004/zaliznyak-konspekt-2015-11-X_bd_t) |

## Stage 2 — Consolidation (intermediate bridge)

| Work | Author · year | Lang | What it is | Read |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| ***Фразы*** (из *Учебника санскритского языка*) | Ф. И. Кнауэр · 1908 | RU | A Russian **exercise-phrase workbook** (lessons `Nr. N (§§ …)`, Devanāgarī + IAST with grammatical footnotes) for **consolidating covered material** — the **bridge between Kochergina and Zaliznyak / Bühler**. Its sentences are cross-referenced to Kochergina & Bühler in the [shared-sentence concordance](https://gasyoun.github.io/SanskritGrammar/grammars/Concordance/catalog). | [Knauer](https://gasyoun.github.io/SanskritGrammar/grammars/KnauerFrazy_1908/Frazy-Knauer-03.05.2023) |

## Stage 3 — Formal morphophonology (post-beginner peak)

The formal Russian grammar layer — where the **Талмуд develops Zaliznyak's classification**:

| Work | Author · year | Lang | What it is | Read |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| ***Грамматический очерк санскрита*** | А. А. Зализняк · 1978 | RU | The founding Russian **formal-structural grammar sketch** (~242 §§): morphonology, positional strong/middle/weak system, ablaut as a calculus. Now **§-addressable** in our site (H241). | [Zaliznyak Очерк](https://gasyoun.github.io/SanskritGrammar/grammars/ZalizniakOcherk_1978/Zaliznyak-Ocherk_29-11-20-aligned) |
| ***Санскритская морфология: руководство*** («Талмуд») | И. Е. Толчельников · 2026 | RU | A generative **Meaning-Text morphology *руководство*** (Whitney-root-based) — computes surface forms from Ряд / Тип / seṭ. Being built into the interactive companion; backs [`samskrtam.ru/z/`](https://samskrtam.ru/z/). **Develops** Zaliznyak's 1975 *Morphophonological Classification* (via Gasuns 2014) — see [`MORPHOCLASS_COMPARISON_ROADMAP.md`](https://github.com/gasyoun/SanskritGrammar/blob/main/MORPHOCLASS_COMPARISON_ROADMAP.md). | [Талмуд (folder)](https://github.com/gasyoun/SanskritGrammar/tree/main/TolchelnikovTalmud_2026) |

---

**The ladder in one line.** Start: **Кочергина + Зализняк *Конспект*** (RU, day one) →
consolidate: **Кнауэр *Фразы*** (RU) → formal peak: **Зализняк *Очерк* + Толчельников
Талмуд** (RU) → advanced: **Bühler** (DE) → **Apte** (EN). The Талмуд develops Zaliznyak's
1975 classification (via Gasuns 2014) — deep comparison planned in
[`MORPHOCLASS_COMPARISON_ROADMAP.md`](https://github.com/gasyoun/SanskritGrammar/blob/main/MORPHOCLASS_COMPARISON_ROADMAP.md).
