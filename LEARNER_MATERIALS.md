# Russian-language Sanskrit learner materials — inventory by track

_Created: 08-07-2026 · Last updated: 08-07-2026_

Which works in this repo are **Russian-language manuals for Sanskrit students**, and
at what level. Companion to the archive listing in
[`README.md`](https://github.com/gasyoun/SanskritGrammar/blob/main/README.md) (which
groups by directory/format); this one groups by **reader track**, per MG's framing:
Kochergina is the complete-beginner course; Zaliznyak and Tolchelnikov are post-beginner.

Every work below is fully digitized as `.mdx` and rendered on the
[Docusaurus site](https://gasyoun.github.io/SanskritGrammar/) (deploy target moving to
samskrtam.ru).

## Track 1 — Complete beginner (zero Sanskrit)

| Work | Author · year | What it is | Digitized | Read |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| ***Учебник санскрита*** | В. А. Кочергина · 1994 (repo carries the 6-е изд. 2017, ред. Н. П. Лихушина) | The standard Russian university **textbook** — graded lessons + grammar + chrestomathy + glossary. Explicitly built for taught **or self-study** use ("под руководством преподавателя или самостоятельно"), and can be started **without** deванāгарī. THE Russian beginner course. | `.mdx`, 124 grid tables | [Kochergina](https://gasyoun.github.io/SanskritGrammar/grammars/KocherginaUchebnik_1998/Kochergina_unicode) |
| ***Фразы*** (из *Учебника санскритского языка*) | Ф. И. Кнауэр · 1908 | Russian **exercise-phrase workbook** — lessons `Nr. N (§§ …)`, each a set of Devanāgarī + IAST sentences with grammatical footnotes keyed to a grammar's §§. A beginner **drill companion** to a grammar, not a standalone course. Its sentences are cross-referenced to Bühler & Kochergina in the [shared-sentence concordance](https://gasyoun.github.io/SanskritGrammar/grammars/Concordance/catalog). | `.mdx` (prose) | [Knauer](https://gasyoun.github.io/SanskritGrammar/grammars/KnauerFrazy_1908/Frazy-Knauer-03.05.2023) |

## Track 2 — Post-beginner (reads Devanāgarī + basic grammar)

| Work | Author · year | What it is | Digitized | Read |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| ***Грамматический очерк санскрита*** | А. А. Зализняк · 1978 | The founding Russian **formal-structural grammar sketch** (~242 §§): morphonology, positional strong/middle/weak system, ablaut as a calculus. A terse **reference**, not a first course. Now **§-addressable** in our site (per-§ anchors, H241). | `.mdx`, 55 tables, §-anchored | [Zaliznyak Очерк](https://gasyoun.github.io/SanskritGrammar/grammars/ZalizniakOcherk_1978/Zaliznyak-Ocherk_29-11-20-aligned) |
| ***Конспект*** (грамматики санскрита) | А. А. Зализняк · 2004 | A dense **conspectus / synopsis** (PIE→OIA sound laws, morphology in brief) — an advanced **revision aid**, not a first course. | `.mdx`, 6 tables | [Zaliznyak Конспект](https://gasyoun.github.io/SanskritGrammar/grammars/ZalizniakKonspekt_2004/zaliznyak-konspekt-2015-11-X_bd_t) |
| ***Санскритская морфология: руководство*** («Талмуд санскрита») | И. Е. Толчельников · 2026 | A generative **Meaning-Text morphology *руководство*** (literally "manual"), Whitney-root-based — computes surface forms from Ряд / Тип / seṭ coordinates. Being built into an **interactive companion to Zaliznyak** (widgets, §-concordance); backs the [`samskrtam.ru/z/`](https://samskrtam.ru/z/) root database. | `.mdx` + interactive widgets | [Талмуд (folder)](https://github.com/gasyoun/SanskritGrammar/tree/main/TolchelnikovTalmud_2026) |

## Russian, but research/reference — NOT student manuals

| Work | Author · year | What it is |
|---|---|---|
| ***Морфонологическая запись глагольных корней санскрита*** | М. Гасунс · 2014 | PhD **dissertation** on root morphonology — the theory behind the Ряд / Тип apparatus. Advanced research, not a course. [folder](https://github.com/gasyoun/SanskritGrammar/tree/main/GasunsDhatu_2014) |
| *Morphophonological Classification of Sanskrit Verbal Roots* | А. А. Зализняк · 1975 | **English-language** research paper; the origin of the Ряд / Тип classification the Talmud runs on. (Russian author, English text.) |

## Non-Russian works in this repo (for completeness — excluded above)

- **Bühler, *Leitfaden für den elementaren Cursus des Sanskrit*** (1923) — **German** elementary course. [read](https://gasyoun.github.io/SanskritGrammar/grammars/BuhlerLeitfaden_1923/Buhler_Unicode)
- **Apte, *Sanskrit Syntax*** (1885) — **English** syntax reference. [read](https://gasyoun.github.io/SanskritGrammar/grammars/ApteSyntax_1885/Apte-unicode)

---

**Short answer.** Yes. For a **complete beginner**, the Russian manual is **Kochergina's
*Учебник санскрита*** (with **Knauer's *Фразы*** as an exercise companion). For a
**post-beginner**, the Russian references are **Zaliznyak** (*Очерк* 1978 + *Конспект*
2004) and **Tolchelnikov's *руководство* / Талмуд** (2026).
