## Data availability

The article-specific evidence release is
[`a61-history-v1.0`](https://github.com/sanskrit-lexicon/csl-observatory/tree/main/data/publication/a61-history-v1.0)
(calendar alias `2026-07`). Its manifest locks the local input artifacts by
SHA-256; `metrics.csv` defines each population and cutoff; `milestones.csv`
separates verified events from pending email evidence; and
`contradictions.csv` records rejected stale forms.

Every quantitative claim in this report cites a committed, versioned dataset. The principal datasets and their homes:

- Union headword index (323,425 headwords), pairwise overlap matrix, and markup-tag census: https://github.com/gasyoun/SanskritLexicography
- Correction-event ledger (52,498 events, 43 dictionaries, 208 release-safe corrector labels) and organisation metrics: https://github.com/sanskrit-lexicon/csl-observatory
- Correction loci and change-file audit trail: https://github.com/sanskrit-lexicon/csl-corrections
- Citation graph (828,505 citations, 912 texts), Harivaṃśa concordance, and Mahābhārata citation census: https://github.com/gasyoun/csl-atlas
- Etymology root oracle and cross-dictionary agreement tables: https://github.com/sanskrit-lexicon/csl-orig (v02/etymology_stats)
- MW–Sanskrit Heritage crosswalk and DCS–CDSL lemma crosswalk: https://github.com/gasyoun/SanskritLexicography and https://github.com/sanskrit-lexicon/csl-apidev
- Whitney roots FAIR crosswalk (CSV/RDF/SQLite): https://github.com/gasyoun/WhitneyRoots
- Headword normalization pipeline: https://github.com/sanskrit-lexicon/hwnorm1
- Dictionary–corpus concordance and the machine-readable dataset manifest (checksums, licence tiers, rebuild recipes): https://github.com/gasyoun/kosha

The dictionary source texts themselves are at https://github.com/sanskrit-lexicon/csl-orig under the Creative Commons terms declared in each dictionary's TEI header.
