# MumbaiWSC_2027 — CDSL report, deep remake

_Created: 12-07-2026 · Last updated: 12-07-2026_

Chapters 00–11 in this folder are the working draft of the CDSL report for the 20th World Sanskrit Conference (Mumbai, December 2027), Computational Sanskrit and Digital Humanities section.

## Provenance

- **Predecessor:** the report submitted to the 19th WSC (Kathmandu, 2025), rejected with four reviews. The as-submitted text is archived at [`papers/KatmanduWSC_2025/Report_on_Cologne_Digital_Sanskrit_Lexicon_Project.mdx`](https://github.com/gasyoun/SanskritGrammar/blob/main/TolchelnikovTalmud_2026/papers/KatmanduWSC_2025/Report_on_Cologne_Digital_Sanskrit_Lexicon_Project.mdx) in this repository.
- **Sibling submissions (different venue, different genre):** the first-person narrative history (A13, [`article/00-report-narrative.md`](https://github.com/sanskrit-lexicon/csl-observatory/blob/main/article/00-report-narrative.md)) and the empirical companion (A14, [`article/01-empirical-companion.md`](https://github.com/sanskrit-lexicon/csl-observatory/blob/main/article/01-empirical-companion.md)) in csl-observatory, both aimed at the Indo-Iranian Journal. This WSC version is the formal-register, data-driven single text; it deliberately drops the personal voice the IIJ pair keeps.
- **Revision sources** (raw files untracked by repository policy, kept in the local `papers/Gasuns-Conferences/Report on Cologne Digital Sanskrit Lexicon Project 2025/` folder): the four WSC-2025 reviews (annotated PDF); Peter Scharf's written review and his archive of funded-phase datasets; the public Cologne volunteer call of 27 June 2026 (recording: https://youtu.be/42MIezNqj5w).

## What changed against the 2025 submission

1. Register lifted from personal account/manifesto to formal academic prose throughout; all four reviews addressed point by point.
2. Every load-bearing quantitative claim now cites a committed dataset (union headword index, correction ledger, markup census, citation graph, crosswalks); data-availability statement added.
3. Scharf's corrections incorporated: Sanskrit Library co-history, the settled merge decision, the TEI history, the 2013 morphological-tag coordination, the Pāṇinian headword-coordination alternative, the actual licence status.
4. Team history updated through Funderburk's June-2026 retirement, cited to the recorded call as an oral-history source.
5. Proper bibliography (Ingalls fixed, Gasūns 2006 supplied, Kapp–Malten completed) and a full abbreviations appendix.
