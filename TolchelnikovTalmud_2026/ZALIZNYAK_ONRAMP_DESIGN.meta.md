## Purpose
Design record for the beginner on-ramp to the Zaliznyak/Tolchelnikov Ряд/Тип/seṭ system — the
gentler tier below the full *Талмуд*. Wave-1c of the digital-pedagogy field (§3.6), H915.

## Audience
Whoever extends or reviews the on-ramp; a fresh session picking up the "Zaliznyak-made-learnable" aspect.

- Prototype: 4 pages, 3 concepts (grades / type / seṭ); reduplication, samprasāraṇa, the nominal system are out of scope by design (deferred to the Талмуд).
- Reuses the full widgets (minus AblautMachine's row filter); `SetTree` still lists all 106 roots.
- No learning-gain evidence yet (that is RQ4/A32).
